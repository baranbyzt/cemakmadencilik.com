How We Extract Manganese

Its symbol is Mn. It was discovered in 1774. It is located in group 7-B of the periodic table. It is grayish metal in color. It is an alloying element that improves the strength of steel. This feature depends on the amount of carbon in it. In high carbon steels, the effect of manganese increases hardness and strength. The periodic structure used for the elements is named A B.

It is generally found in nature in conjunction with the element iron and many other elements. It is generally used in alloys in the metal industry, especially in stainless steel making, it is a necessary raw material in the alloy. It is named after the Magnesia region of Greece. It was first obtained from the manganese dioxide compound in 1774 as a result of the reduction of carbon. It is used to prevent corrosion and rust in steel.

Zinc is an important element used in the manufacture of carbon and alkaline batteries. Manganese ions can be found in a wide variety of enzymes and photosynthetic plants. High levels of manganese are toxic to mammals.
