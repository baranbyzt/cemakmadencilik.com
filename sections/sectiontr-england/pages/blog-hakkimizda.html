About Çemak

History of Cemak Mining

Cemak Mining Industry and Trade Inc. is a metallic mining group with businesses, projects and explorations in precious and base metals. Our strategy is to conduct advanced searches in potential mine sites without compromising our quality, while respecting nature, the environment and people; is to be among the important gold producers of the country with its sustainable mining projects that integrate economic and social development with environmental protection, in economic terms and on a large scale. In this sense, our company holds many mineral exploration licenses. Çemak Madencilik aims sustainable gold mining with high economic returns, with a transparent, open, honest management approach and communication, respectful to values ​​and the environment, by ensuring the health and safety of its employees in all its operations and exploration sites, in mutual trust with the society, stakeholders and employees. . All our mining operations and projects; It has the best applicable technologies as it includes all components of OHS, Environment, Community Relations, Quality and Integrated Management systems, as well as national and international standards. Before the mining activities start, in the light of the basic data and information obtained as a result of the studies carried out by the experts on air, water, soil quality, plant-animal species, cultural and social assets; All environmental and social values ​​will be preserved in production, recycling, and processes. Çemak aims to provide support to the activities of the local people, based on mutual trust, respecting values, open and transparent communication in the regions where mining will be carried out, with social approval, complaint and feedback, stakeholder participation, local purchasing, local employment understanding and management, protecting the environment and protecting the economic and social environment. will provide development.
