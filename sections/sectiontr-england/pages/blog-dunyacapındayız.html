We are Worldwide

As Cemak Mining, we export mining raw materials to countries such as China, Rusia, Turkey, United States with our worldwide mining infrastructure. We export our most needed mines Gold, Iron, Manganese, Coal and many other raw materials to the whole world. Thanks to the wide infrastructure we have, we deliver our raw materials in a timely and appropriate amount to the companies of the countries we have an agreement with. We meet the needs.

THE FUNCTIONAL SCHEME FROM THE MINE EXTRACTING TO THE LAST STAGE IS AS FOLLOWS.

AFTER THE EXCAVATOR IS REMOVED WITH CRUSHER AND BUCKET, IT IS LOADED TO THE TRUCKS WITH A LOADER, THEN IT IS BROUGHT TO THE CRUSHER AND THE CRUSHING PROCESS IS APPLIED, AT 0,10 DEGREES BREAKED MATERIAL IS SEPARATED BY WATER AND AIR METHOD IN THE JIG UNIT (ENRICHING) AND THE ORE IS OUT. THE ORE OPERATED AS TWO TYPES 1.SI THE SECOND GRAIN THAT WE CALL HAZELNUT IS THE POWDER STATE OF THE ORE WE CALLED CONCENTRATE. OUTPUT MATERIAL IS SEPARATED INTO 3 POOLS UNDER THE JIG UNIT, THESE ARE HAZELNUT, IT IS WASTE THAT WE CALLED CONCENTRATE AND PASHA. THE BUYER AFTER WAIT TO DRY FOR A TIME OF DRYING IN THE FIELD OF THE SEPARATED HAZELNUT AND CONCENTRATED STOCK TENOR QUALITY SILICONE AND MOISTURE RATIO CAN BE LEARNED BY PERFORMING ANALYSIS OF THE COMPANY, CALLED SGS. IT IS SHIPPED TO BUYER COUNTRIES.
